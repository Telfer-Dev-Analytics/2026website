Our Mission

BTA bridges the gap between business strategy and technology. We help members understand how tech drives growth, innovation, and efficiency in the modern workplace.

◇

What We Do

Workshops, speaker events, hackathons, and networking. We explore AI, data, automation, and digital transformation—always with a business lens.

○

Who It's For

Students and professionals who care about the intersection of business and tech. No coding required—curiosity and ambition are enough.

FAQ

How do I join BTA?

Come to any of our events or sign up through our membership form. We welcome everyone interested in business and technology.

Do I need a tech background?

No. BTA is for anyone curious about how technology shapes business. We cover concepts in an accessible way and offer beginner-friendly workshops.

Is there a membership fee?

Membership is free for students. Some special events or workshops may have a small fee to cover materials or venue costs.

How can I get involved as a volunteer or leader?

Reach out to our exec team at events or via email. We often look for event helpers, content creators, and future committee members.

Where are events held?

Most events are on campus. Check each event listing for the exact room or venue. We also host occasional virtual sessions.
